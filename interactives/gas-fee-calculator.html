What is Gwei?

Gwei is a small unit of ETH (1 Gwei = 0.000000001 ETH). Gas prices are measured in Gwei per gas unit. Higher Gwei = faster transaction but more expensive.

Tips to Save Gas

Gas prices fluctuate based on network demand. Try transacting during off-peak hours (weekends, early morning) for lower fees. Or use Layer 2 networks like Arbitrum or Polygon!
